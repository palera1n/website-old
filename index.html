palera1n

palera1n is a developer-oriented jailbreak for checkm8 devices (A8-A11) on iOS 15.0-16.3.

Get it now!

Frequently asked questions

  • How can I jailbreak?
    The following steps on ios.cfw.guide can help you.
  • What platforms are supported?
    You may jailbreak any A8-A11 device on iOS 15.0-16.3 using macOS or Linux.
  • Can I use my tweaks?
    Yes, we have tweak support, but only tweaks that work on iOS 15-16 will work, there's no guarantee that iOS 14 and lower tweaks will work.
  • What is rootless?
    Rootless is the future of iOS 15+ jailbreaks. As of now, palera1n has full root support, at the expense of 5-10 GB of storage (or tethered). We aim to switch to rootless, to also bring a smoother experience.
